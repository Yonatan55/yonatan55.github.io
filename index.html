About me

Hello there! I’m a researcher with a deep passion for applied mathematics. My work focuses on stochastic modeling, differential equations, and mathematical biology, often intersecting with data science and machine learning. I enjoy exploring how mathematical tools can address complex challenges in science and engineering. This page is a space where I share my projects, insights, and enthusiasm for the beauty and utility of mathematics.

Research & Projects

My research interests include analysis of small active particles in fluids and applying data science techniques like reinforcement learning and likelihood-based inference. I don't always write code but when I do I use Python and MATLAB.

My papers with collaborators are listed here: Google Scholar.

A preprint is found here: Stochastic Analysis of Taxis and Kinesis Properties of Colonial Protozoa (PDF)

My current projects are:
  • Bifurcations and mobility statistics of gliding behavior in diatoms
  • Hidden Markov based inference for intracellular dynamics
